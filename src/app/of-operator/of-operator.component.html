Of-operator makes a variable into an Observable. Open the console to see Array, string and Object -based Observables subscribed and printed out.

From-operator makes an Array-like variable or a Promise into an Observable. As seen from the console, Of-induced Observable prints the whole array, while From-induced Observable prints the data one element at a time.
